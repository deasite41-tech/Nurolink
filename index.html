About Nurolink
Advanced Cognitive Assessment Platform

NUROLINK is a cognitive assessment platform powered by advanced neural technology and clinical research. Designed to evaluate function across multiple critical domains, NUROLINK provides comprehensive brain health screening that goes beyond traditional assessments.

Our platform leverages cutting-edge AI and validated neuropsychological testing methodologies to deliver real-time performance analysis. The assessment suite includes four specialized modules that together build a complete cognitive risk profile.

Each module employs adaptive difficulty scaling, adjusting challenge levels in real-time based on performance. Response times, accuracy rates, and performance trajectories are continuously tracked to build a complete cognitive picture.

NUROLINK synthesizes individual domain performance into a comprehensive Alzheimer's Risk Report. All assessments are performed locally on your device with no external data transmission, ensuring complete privacy while maintaining research-grade integrity.
